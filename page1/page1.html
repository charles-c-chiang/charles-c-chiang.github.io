Mad Libs

Let me explain how they work. First, I'll ask for some part of a sentence.

Your job is to fill in a word (any word!) that fits that part of speech.

For example, I could ask for an adjective. Your job would be to say something like, "creamy".

At the end, I'll put the words into a story and then you'll see what you've helped create!

We'll start with a small example. Remember, be creative and have fun!
